the baker

Hi, I'm Mckenna.

I trained at Le Cordon Bleu and fell in love with the patient, fussy little details of French pâtisserie — then brought all of it home to my own kitchen in Boise.

Everything is small-batch and made the slow way: real butter, good fruit, and recipes I've tinkered with until they taste like a hug. Whether it's a box of muffins for a slow morning or a cake for the people you love, I'd be so happy to bake it for you.
