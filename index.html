About Mckenna's Baked Goods

A bakery with a quiet patisserie mood.

Mckenna graduated from Le Cordon Bleu in London in 2024. She is a master of French cuisine and baking. Fusing French pâtisserie technique with American home-style recipes, we create bakes that are cozy to eat and polished enough for any celebration.
