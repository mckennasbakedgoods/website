Who's baking this

I trained at Le Cordon Bleu in London, fell in love with the fussy little details of French pâtisserie, then brought all of it home to my kitchen here in Boise.

Everything is small-batch and made the slow way: real butter, good fruit, and recipes I've tinkered with until they taste like a hug. my favorite part is the boxing

Whether it's a box of muffins for a slow morning or a cake for the people you love, I'd be so glad to bake it for you.
