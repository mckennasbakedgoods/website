Fresh from the kitchen

Hi, I'm Mckenna.

I trained at Le Cordon Bleu, fell in love with the fussy little details of French pâtisserie, then brought all of it home to my kitchen in Boise.

Everything is small-batch and made the slow way: real butter, good fruit, and recipes I've tinkered with until they taste like a hug. Whether it's a box of muffins for a slow morning or a cake for the people you love, I'd be glad to bake it for you.
